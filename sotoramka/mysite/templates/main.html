HONEY FRAME PACKAGING

__________

Our team is engaged in the development and production of packaging for honeycombs

  • 435mm x 145mm - half comb frame
  • 435mm x 230mm - langstroth frame
  • 435mm x 300mm - dadant frame

PRODUCTION DETAILS

__________

The packaging is a cardboard box made of micro-cardboard of the original design, which allows you to fix the honeycomb frame in the middle of the box, so that it does not touch the walls of the box with a honey seal.

For convinience purposes, our packages are equipped with carrying a handle

On the sides of the box there are easily removable transparent panes. They are designed for preliminary demonstration of the honeycomb frame without opening the box

The package is provided with a polypropylene film insert to protect the honeycomb frame from dust. It is suitable for contact with food products

The box-packaging is delivered in following sets:

  • 50 items per set - half comb frame
  • 40 items per set - langstroth frame
  • 25 items per set - dadant frame

Shipping is performed by Transport Companies

The package is delivered at the expense of the customer
